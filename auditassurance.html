Audit & Assurance Services You Can Trust

At Shepherd Assurance, we provide a comprehensive range of Audit & Assurance services designed to deliver clarity, transparency, and confidence in your financial reporting. Whether you're a small business, a large corporation, a charity, or a legal practice, our expert team ensures that your financial statements are accurate, compliant, and reliable.

Our Audit and Assurance Services Include:

Statutory Audits

Our statutory audits provide an independent and thorough examination of your company’s financial statements, ensuring they accurately reflect the true financial position of your business. We help you meet all legal and regulatory requirements while delivering clarity and confidence in your financial reporting. Our audits offer assurance to shareholders, investors, and regulators that your financial statements are compliant and reliable, supporting your business’s credibility and trustworthiness.

Gross Turnover Audits (GTO)

Our Gross Turnover Audits meticulously assess the accuracy of your reported turnover, ensuring it aligns with your financial records and contractual agreements. We help identify any inconsistencies or errors, reducing the risk of financial discrepancies, non-compliance, and penalties. By ensuring that your turnover figures are precise and reliable, we provide the confidence and clarity needed to support your business's financial integrity and compliance.

Learn More
Solicitor Audits

We specialize in auditing legal practices to ensure compliance with the Solicitors Accounts Rules. Our services focus on safeguarding client funds, strengthening financial controls, and ensuring adherence to all regulatory requirements. By conducting thorough audits, we help maintain the integrity of your practice, providing assurance to both clients and regulatory bodies that your financial practices are transparent, compliant, and secure.

Charity Audits

We provide specialized audit services for non-profit organizations, ensuring compliance with legal and regulatory requirements. Our audits promote transparency in financial reporting, verify the proper use of funds, and confirm adherence to Charity Commission guidelines. By partnering with us, your charity can maintain accountability, build trust with donors, and protect its reputation, ensuring long-term sustainability and continued support.

Grant Audit Services

Our grant audit services assist organizations in confirming that grant funds are being utilized according to the terms set out in grant agreements and the expectations of donors. We offer independent verification that funds are allocated correctly and in line with their intended purposes, ensuring compliance with financial regulations. Our audits help reduce risks, strengthen accountability, and increase transparency, providing organizations with the confidence that they are meeting donor requirements and maintaining trust.

Agreed Upon Procedures (AUP)

We conduct tailored procedures as agreed upon with you, delivering a comprehensive report on our findings. AUP engagements are ideal for situations that require limited assurance, such as internal investigations, compliance audits of contracts, or evaluations of financial performance. Our focused approach ensures that specific concerns are addressed with clarity and precision, providing you with actionable insights and confidence in your decision-making.
