Individual Tax Services

We assist in providing these services that cater to individuals who need assistance with personal income taxes, tax planning, and ensuring compliance with the tax code.

Key Benefits of Our Individual Tax Services:
  • Income Tax Filing: We work with you to define specific procedures that address your unique concerns and objectives, ensuring that the engagement is relevant to your needs.
  • Tax Planning: Strategizing on how to structure income, investments, and deductions to minimize taxes over time. This can involve retirement planning, estate planning, and charitable giving strategies.
  • Tax Advisory: Professional advice on personal financial matters, such as tax implications of large purchases, inheritances, or major life changes (e.g., marriage, divorce). Guidance on tax-efficient investments and tax-saving opportunities.
  • Tax Audit Representation: Providing representation if the individual is selected for an audit by tax authorities. Helping respond to audit inquiries, and negotiating on behalf of the client to resolve tax disputes.

Business Tax Services

Businesses require specialized tax services to ensure they comply with corporate tax regulations and optimize their financial performance.

Key Benefits of Our Business Tax Services:
  • Corporate Tax Filing: Preparation and filing of corporate tax returns (C-Corporations, S-Corporations, LLCs, partnerships). Ensuring accurate reporting of business profits, deductions, credits, and liabilities.
  • Sales and Use Tax: Guidance on sales tax obligations for businesses, especially in states with varying tax laws. Assistance in understanding use tax requirements and ensuring compliance with local tax regulations.
  • Payroll Tax Compliance: Ensuring businesses with employees comply with payroll tax requirements (income tax withholding, social security, Medicare, unemployment taxes). Filing payroll returns and remitting required payments.
  • Tax Planning for Businesses: Structuring the business in a way that minimizes tax exposure, such as choosing the right legal entity (LLC, corporation, etc.). Guidance on maximizing deductions, credits, and tax-deferred retirement plans for businesses.
  • Transfer Pricing: Ensuring that businesses engaged in international trade comply with transfer pricing rules (pricing of goods/services between related entities across borders). Preparing documentation for compliance and reducing the risk of tax adjustments.
  • Tax Credits and Incentives: Identifying tax credits (e.g., R&D tax credits, energy tax credits) and other incentives businesses may qualify for. Helping businesses claim credits and incentives to reduce tax liabilities.
  • State and Local Tax Services: Providing guidance on the taxation of businesses at the state and local levels, including sales tax, property tax, and franchise tax. Offering support for multi-state businesses with varying tax obligations across jurisdictions.

International Tax Solutions

Global businesses and individuals with international financial interests face complex tax situations that require expert advice.

Key Benefits of Our International Tax Solutions:
  • Cross-Border Tax Planning: Assistance for businesses and individuals dealing with tax obligations in multiple countries. Developing tax strategies to minimize double taxation through the use of tax treaties, foreign tax credits, and other mechanisms.
  • International Tax Compliance: Ensuring compliance with the tax laws of foreign countries and the United States for U.S. businesses operating overseas or foreign entities operating in the U.S. Preparing tax returns, reporting foreign income, and adhering to FATCA (Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act) requirements.
  • Expatriate Tax Services: Providing tax advice for individuals living abroad or expats moving to the U.S. Ensuring compliance with foreign income reporting requirements, including the Foreign Earned Income Exclusion (FEIE) and tax treaty benefits.
  • Transfer Pricing Advisory: Helping multinational corporations comply with transfer pricing regulations for cross-border transactions between related entities. Providing support for documentation and dispute resolution with tax authorities.
  • Tax Structuring for International Business: Advising businesses on the most tax-efficient ways to structure operations internationally, including considering tax treaties, holding company arrangements, and financing options.
